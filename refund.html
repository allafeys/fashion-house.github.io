Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Please read this refund policy carefully before making any purchases on our website.

1. General Refund Policy

At KLF, we strive to provide high-quality products/services and excellent customer service. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer the following refund options:

  • Digital Products: Refund requests must be made within 30 days of purchase
  • Physical Products: Refund requests must be made within 30 days of receiving the product
  • Services: Refund requests must be made within 30 days of service completion

2. Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, your item must meet the following requirements:

  • The product must be unused and in the same condition as when you received it
  • The product must be in its original packaging
  • You must provide proof of purchase (receipt or order number)
  • For services, work must not have been substantially completed

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Downloadable software products
  • Gift cards
  • Products marked as "final sale" or "non-refundable"
  • Services that have been fully completed
  • Custom or personalized orders

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at aliyakadyrova06@gmil.com within the refund period
  2. Include your order number and reason for the refund request
  3. For physical products, you may be required to ship the item back to us
  4. Once received, we will inspect the item and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund

5. Refund Processing

If your refund is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 30 business days
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive a confirmation email when the refund has been processed

Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider.

6. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe:

  1. Check your bank account again
  2. Contact your credit card company or payment provider
  3. If you've done all of this and still haven't received your refund, please contact us at aliyakadyrova06@gmil.com

7. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item, contact us at aliyakadyrova06@gmil.com and send the item to our return address.

8. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
